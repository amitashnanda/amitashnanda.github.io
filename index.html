About Me

I am a graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Instrumentation and Electronics Engineering and currently working as a Researcher in Accenture Research Lab. I love to learn new technologies and apply them to solve real-life problems. I enjoy exploring different Engineering fields, and my interests include Electronics, the Internet of Things, Robotics, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Wireless communication, and Embedded System. I spend most of my time learning and building projects.
I am quite focused on the things I do and give all my efforts to complete the same. In my free time, I used to research various problems the society is facing and try to develop some innovative ideas. Researching particular stuff excites me and helps me to learn a lot of things.
